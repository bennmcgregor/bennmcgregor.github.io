Music Without Borders

I co-created the Music Without Borders fundraising campaign, which draws on the power of the arts to support the humanitarian work of Médecins Sans Frontières / Doctors Without Borders (MSF). It's a completely student-run project that has raised more than $56,500 and engaged hundreds of youth since 2014. It continues to engage high school students from diverse interests and backgrounds across North America.

To find out more about the story of Music Without Borders, check out this article that I wrote for MSF Canada.

Visit our website at musicwithoutborder.com.
